# Privacy Policy **App Name:** Packing List Weight Tracker **Last updated:** 20.05.2026 ## Overview This Privacy Policy describes how Packing List Weight Tracker ("the App", "we", "our") handles your information. The App is developed and operated by Savye ("the Developer"). The App is designed to minimize data collection. It does not require an account, does not collect personal information, and stores your packing lists only on your device. ## Data You Create in the App All content you create in the App — packing lists, items, weights, and notes — is stored **locally on your device** using the iOS system storage (AsyncStorage). This data: - Is never sent to our servers - Is not synchronized to any cloud service by the App - Is included in your device backups (iCloud or local) if you have those enabled in iOS settings - Is deleted when you delete the App We have no access to this data and cannot retrieve, restore, or view it. You can write anything into a packing list, including text that could be considered personal. Because this content stays on your device and is never transmitted to us, we never receive or process it. ## In-App Purchase (RevenueCat) The App offers a one-time purchase of **€7.99** to unlock full functionality. This purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store. We use **RevenueCat** to verify your purchase status and manage entitlements. When you make a purchase or open the App, RevenueCat receives: - A pseudonymous user identifier generated by the App (not linked to your name or email) - Device identifiers such as IDFV (Identifier for Vendor) - Transaction data from Apple (purchase status, receipt validation) RevenueCat does not receive your name, email address, or App Store account details. Apple handles the actual payment and does not share your payment information with us. - RevenueCat privacy policy: - Apple privacy policy: The legal basis for this processing under the GDPR is the **performance of a contract** (Article 6(1)(b)) — we need to verify your purchase to provide the features you paid for. ## App Store Review Prompts The App may occasionally ask you to rate it on the App Store using Apple's standard review prompt (`SKStoreReviewController`). This prompt is displayed and handled entirely by iOS — we do not see whether you responded, what rating you gave, or whether you wrote a review. Apple controls when and how often this prompt appears. ## What We Do Not Collect To be explicit, the App does **not** collect, use, or share: - Personal information such as name, email, or phone number - Location data - Contacts, photos, calendar, or other device content - Analytics or usage tracking data - Advertising identifiers for advertising purposes - Crash reports (beyond Apple's standard, anonymous App Store mechanisms) The App does not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. ## Apple's Independent Data Collection Apple Inc., as the operator of the App Store, may independently collect data related to App Store transactions, downloads, and anonymous crash reports. This collection is governed by Apple's privacy policy and is not controlled by us. You can manage some of these settings under **Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements** on your device. ## Children's Privacy The App is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Because the App does not collect personal data from any user, no data about children is collected. ## Your Rights (GDPR) Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have rights including: - **Right of access** — to know what personal data we hold about you - **Right to rectification** — to correct inaccurate data - **Right to erasure** — to have your data deleted - **Right to data portability** — to receive your data in a portable format - **Right to lodge a complaint** with your local data protection authority (in Finland: ) Because we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no personal data we hold about you that we could access, correct, or delete on your behalf. Data stored locally on your device can be deleted at any time by deleting the App from your device. To exercise rights regarding purchase data held by RevenueCat or Apple, please contact those parties directly. ## International Transfers RevenueCat and Apple may process data in countries outside the European Economic Area, including the United States. These transfers are governed by their own privacy policies and appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses. ## Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this document reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated through the App or its App Store listing.
